# LFG Marketing Video Script

Duration: 20 seconds
Format: 16:9 landscape, 1920x1080
Tone: energetic, social, app-forward

## Beat 1: Hook, 0-4s

On-screen: "Fitness accountability that shows up with your friends."

Visual: LFG logo, product screenshots, and quick activity chips establish the product.

## Beat 2: Challenges, 4-8s

On-screen: "Create a challenge. Invite the crew. Set the score to chase."

Visual: A challenge setup panel advances through goal, duration, invite, and start states.

## Beat 3: Notifications, 8-12s

On-screen: "Your friends keep you moving."

Visual: Friend notification cards slide in around the real LFG notification asset.

## Beat 4: Leaderboard, 12-16s

On-screen: "Every workout moves the leaderboard."

Visual: Ranks and score bars animate as friends climb the challenge leaderboard.

## Beat 5: CTA, 16-20s

On-screen: "Download LFG. Challenge your friends today."

Visual: App icon, app store badges, and final product lockup.
